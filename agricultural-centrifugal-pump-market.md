<p><p>The agricultural centrifugal pump market primarily consists of single-stage and multi-stage pumps. Single-stage pumps are designed for applications requiring lower pressure and flow rates, making them suitable for irrigation and water transfer tasks. In contrast, multi-stage pumps are ideal for high-pressure applications, such as deep well pumping or elevated irrigation systems, where multiple impellers are used to increase pressure and efficiency. Each type serves distinct agricultural needs, enhancing productivity and resource management in farming operations.</p></p>
