<p><strong>The Flue Gas Cooling Plant Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Direct Cooling</li><li>Indirect Cooling</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The flue gas cooling plant market is primarily divided into two types: direct cooling and indirect cooling. Direct cooling involves the direct contact of flue gas with a cooling medium, leading to efficient heat transfer and reduced gas temperature. In contrast, indirect cooling utilizes heat exchangers to separate the flue gas from the cooling medium, providing enhanced control over emissions and reducing contamination risks. Both methods are essential in optimizing thermal efficiency and minimizing environmental impact in various industrial applications.</p></p>
