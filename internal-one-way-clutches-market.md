<p><ul><li>Roller Design</li><li>Spiral Design</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Internal One-way Clutches market is primarily divided into two design types: Roller and Spiral. Roller design clutches utilize cylindrical components that engage with precision, allowing motion in one direction while preventing reverse movement. In contrast, Spiral design clutches employ a helical mechanism that provides proportional engagement based on load, offering smoother operation. Both designs cater to various applications, including automotive and industrial machinery, providing efficient torque transfer and enhancing overall performance by ensuring reliable one-way motion.</p></p>
