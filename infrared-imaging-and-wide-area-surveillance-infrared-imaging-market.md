<p><p>Infrared imaging and wide area surveillance utilize different infrared spectrum ranges to detect and analyze thermal signatures. Near IR (NIR) is suitable for low-light applications, enhancing visibility in darkness. Short Wave IR (SWIR) detects moisture and material identification. Mid Wave IR (MWIR) is effective for tracking moving targets, often used in military applications. Long Wave IR (LWIR) excels in thermal imaging, providing high sensitivity for heat detection, making it ideal for surveillance and search-and-rescue operations across large areas.</p></p>
