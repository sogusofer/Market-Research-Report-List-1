<p><p>The Automotive Active Axle Steering market is divided into two main types: Active Front Axle Steering and Active Rear Axle Steering. Conversely, Active Rear Axle Steering modifies the rear wheels' angle, enabling better stability and reduced turning radius, particularly at low speeds. Both systems contribute to improved safety, agility, and overall driving experience.</p></p>
