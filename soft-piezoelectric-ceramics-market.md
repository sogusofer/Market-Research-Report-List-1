<p><p>The soft piezoelectric ceramics market primarily includes materials like PZT5A and PZT5H, which are types of lead zirconate titanate (PZT). PZT5A is known for its high sensitivity and lower hysteresis, making it suitable for applications in sensors and actuators. PZT5H offers enhanced performance under high electric fields, making it ideal for ultrasonic devices and transducers. Both materials are critical in various industries, including automotive, medical, and consumer electronics, driving demand for advanced piezoelectric solutions.</p></p>
