<p><ul><li>Clip-On Type</li><li>Adhesive Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The tire balance market primarily consists of two types: clip-on and adhesive weights. Clip-on weights are metal clips that attach to the rim's edge, providing a quick and adjustable solution for balancing tires. They are often preferred for their ease of installation and removal. Adhesive weights, on the other hand, are glued directly to the inside of the rim, offering a more streamlined appearance and reduced risk of damage during handling. Both types play essential roles in ensuring optimal tire performance and safety.</p></p>
