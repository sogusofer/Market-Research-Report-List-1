<p><p>The urban rail signalling systems market includes three main types: Semi-Automatic Train Operation (SATO), where operators assist automated systems for enhanced efficiency; Driverless Train Operation (DTO), which fully automates train control without human intervention, ensuring safety and precision; and Unattended Train Operation (UTO), similar to DTO but focuses specifically on trains operating without onboard staff in stations, relying on advanced technology for monitoring and control. These systems aim to improve reliability, safety, and operational efficiency in urban transit.</p></p>
