<p><p>Arrhythmia management devices encompass pacemakers, defibrillators, and ablation devices, each serving pivotal roles in treating heart rhythm disorders. Pacemakers regulate heartbeats by delivering electrical impulses, ensuring a consistent rhythm. Defibrillators, including implantable and external types, restore normal heart rhythm during life-threatening arrhythmias, primarily through shock delivery. Ablation devices target and eliminate dysfunctional heart tissue causing irregular rhythms via various techniques. Together, these devices significantly enhance patient outcomes and quality of life by effectively managing arrhythmias.</p></p>
