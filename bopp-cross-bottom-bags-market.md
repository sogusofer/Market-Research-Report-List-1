<p><p>BOPP cross bottom bags are designed for bulk packaging and come in two primary types: valve cross bottom bags and open cross bottom bags. Valve cross bottom bags feature a built-in valve that allows for easy filling while minimizing dust and spillage, making them ideal for powders and granules. Open cross bottom bags, on the other hand, have an open top that permits straightforward filling and accessibility, suitable for various bulk materials. Both types offer durability and excellent barrier properties.</p></p>
