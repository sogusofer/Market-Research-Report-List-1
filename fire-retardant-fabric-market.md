<p><strong>The Fire Retardant Fabric Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Inherent Fire Retardant Fabric</li><li>Treated Fire Retardant Fabric</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The fire retardant fabric market is primarily divided into two types: inherent fire retardant fabric and treated fire retardant fabric. Inherent fire retardant fabrics possess flame-resistant properties embedded within the fibers themselves, ensuring long-lasting protection even after washing. In contrast, treated fire retardant fabrics undergo an external chemical treatment to impart flame resistance, which may diminish over time with washing or wear. Both types are essential in industries such as construction, transportation, and protective clothing, prioritizing safety and compliance.</p></p>
