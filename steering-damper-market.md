<p><strong>The Steering Damper Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Rotary Type</li><li>Piston Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The steering damper market is primarily divided into two types: rotary and piston types. Rotary type dampers use a rotating mechanism to absorb shocks and vibrations, providing smooth steering response, commonly found in motorcycles and sports vehicles. In contrast, piston type dampers employ a piston within a cylinder to control fluid flow and resistance, offering enhanced stability and control in various automotive applications. Both types play a critical role in improving vehicle handling and safety, catering to different performance needs.</p></p>
