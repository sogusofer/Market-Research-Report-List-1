<p><p>The air cooling battery rack market includes two primary types: passive air cooling and active air cooling. Passive air cooling relies on natural airflow to dissipate heat from batteries, utilizing ventilation and thermal convection without mechanical assistance. In contrast, active air cooling employs fans or blowers to enhance airflow, actively regulating temperature for optimal battery performance. Both systems aim to improve battery life and efficiency, catering to different operational environments and cooling requirements within energy storage solutions.</p></p>
